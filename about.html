Why Software

[Systems Photo]

What draws me to software isn't just the logic — it's the complexity. I love looking at a system and wondering how someone figured out how to make it all work together. Every framework, every protocol, every tiny library is a solution to a real problem that someone thought through carefully. That kind of layered problem-solving fascinates me. The fact that we can build something so intricate from just text in a file still feels like magic.

That same curiosity pulls me toward other systems too — finance, economics, science. I spend hours watching channels like Veritasium, How Money Works, and similar creators who break down complex ideas into something you can actually understand. Whether it's how markets work, why things go viral, or what makes a bridge stay up — I just like knowing how the pieces fit together. Gaming scratches the same itch; I love games with deep mechanics where you have to figure out the system to get better.
